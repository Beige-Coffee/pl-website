# Pay It Forward

This course was built as a free, open-source educational resource. Every coding exercise, every checkpoint reward, every sat you've earned throughout this course was a real Lightning transaction, made possible by the contributions of previous students and supporters.

If you found this course valuable, consider paying it forward. Your donation directly funds the next wave of students working through these same exercises, earning real sats, and building real intuition for how Lightning works under the hood.

More courses are on the way, and your contribution helps make them just as hands-on.

Every sat counts. Thanks for paying it forward.
